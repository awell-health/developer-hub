Increase Data Quality with Form Validation

You can now add validation rules to short text inputs in Awell’s form builder. Choose from built-in validation options for simple use cases, or define your custom rule using regular expressions for more advanced needs. Ensuring correct input formats reduces errors, improves data quality, and cuts down operational overhead.

Instantly Ship Care Flows – You're in Control

You’re now fully in control. Promote care flows from your Sandbox to Production instantly—no more waiting, no more reaching out to Awell. This puts the release process in your hands, bringing CareOps into full action.

 

It’s a “finally,” and yes—a “sorry it took us this long.”

 

Get started with Promotion.

Granular Access Controls for Publishing and Releasing Care Flows

We’ve introduced more granular access controls to help you manage who can publish and release care flows within your organization.

  • Publishers can publish care flows, making them eligible for release
  • Releasers can set flows live and promote them to Production

These roles can now be assigned and managed directly by organization admins in the Settings section of the platform.

 

Start assigning Roles and Permissions.
