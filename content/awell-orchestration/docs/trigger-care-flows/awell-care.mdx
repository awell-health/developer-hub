**Awell Care is the application built, maintained, and provided by Awell that allows you to Orchestrate and operate the care flows you have designed in Awell Studio.** While most of our customers currently opt for a headless approach to Orchestration, there are still some who utilize Awell Care for handling and overseeing their patient population.
